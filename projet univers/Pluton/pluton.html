The Frozen World

Pluto is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt, beyond Neptune. Though smaller than Earthâ€™s Moon, it has a complex surface with icy plains, mountains made of water ice, and regions covered in nitrogen and methane ice. It also has five known moons, with Charon being the largest, forming a binary system with Pluto.
